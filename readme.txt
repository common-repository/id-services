<p>Allows your WooCommerce store to easily offer a discount for Military, First Responders, Healthcare Workers, Students, Teachers, Government employees and much more.</p>

<p>This plugin automates the full process of installing a page to host your discounts, and releasing a unique, single-use coupon code to your customer upon verification.</p>

<p>VerifyPass is a leading brand in community verification. Serving over 1,300+ merchants, VerifyPass is the simplest solution for automating the process of validating customer eligibility for targeted promotions.</p>

**How does verification work?**

<p>Customers verify their community affiliation via VerifyPass authoritative data sources and verification staff. The process typically happens in under 30 seconds. There is no account required for your customers to use VerifyPass.</p>

**Who can be verified?**

<p>Select from 90+ Communities, such as the Military, First Responders, Teachers, Healthcare Workers, and much more. Learn more about our communities, and how we verify them: <a href=https://verifypass.com/verification/communities>https://verifypass.com/verification/communities</a></p>

**What happens after verification?**

<p>After verification, customers receive a unique, single-use coupon code to your store. Advanced stores can setup to not use discount codes, taking full control over the customer journey. With a custom integration, you could create a Pro Program and apply discounts automatically. Or, restrict parts of your website to verified customers. With custom integrations, there are no restrictions on what you can build.</p>

== Installation ==

1. Upload unzipped `id-services` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Ensure <a href="http://www.woothemes.com/woocommerce/" target="_blank">WooCommerce</a> is installed.
4. Create a VerifyPass Business Account.
5. Copy/paste your API Key to the plugin.
6. Return to your VerifyPass account to complete setup.
